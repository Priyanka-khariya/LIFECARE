Cardiovascular Domain

Cardiovascular disease is a general term for conditions affecting the heart or blood vessels. It includes coronary heart disease, stroke, peripheral vascular disease, congenital heart disease, endocarditis etc.

Gynaecological Domain

Gynecological disorders are those disorders that affect the female reproductive system. The organs included in reproductive system are breasts, uterus, fallopian tube, ovaries, and external genitalia.

Orthopedic Domain

Branch of medicine concerned with diseases, injuries, and conditions of the musculoskeletal system relating to the body's muscles and skeleton. It is a medical specialty that deals with the treatment of bones and muscle
